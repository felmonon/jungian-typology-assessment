## Weekly Backlink Routine

1. Pick 10 relevant creators/sites from `marketing/launch/creator-outreach.csv`.
2. Add a specific reason each audience fits TypeJung.
3. Send 5 careful emails or DMs per day.
4. Track replies, links earned, and feedback in the CSV.
